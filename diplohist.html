Introduction and Context: The Seven Years War

This is where the diplomatic history assignment is. Incorporating several types of sources, including images, equivalently long text, and other potential forms of academic enrichment, I would explore and develop the thesis using this medium.

There is much disagreement among historians as to what was the most influential cause of the American and French Revolutions, including ideology, the Seven Years War, and various financial and taxational factors. However, the vital event and cause of the revolutions was the Seven Years War and its effects, which led largely to the other debated causes by drastically affecting the European as well as North American state of affairs.
