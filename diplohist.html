Introduction and Context: The Seven Years War

This is where the diplomatic history assignment is. Incorporating several types of sources, including images, equivalently long text, and other potential forms of academic enrichment, I would explore and develop the thesis using this medium.

While there is much disagreement among historians, it is clear that the Seven Years War had the most direct and profound impact on creating the American and French Revolutions, through its direct effects on patriotism, diplomacy, and taxation, as well as its role in creating the other factors which historians have recognized as causes for the revolutions.
